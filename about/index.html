Henri Bergius

Henri Bergius, a.k.a. Bergie, is a former Viking originally from the Nordic country of Finland but living in Berlin. When he is not exploring Georgiaâ€™s cave cities or running with the bulls in Pamplona, Bergie works on a new generation of web editing interfaces with Create.js and Flow-Based Programming with NoFlo.

After half a decade of regular web development, Bergie got involved with free software in 1999 by coordinating the public release of the Midgard content management system.
