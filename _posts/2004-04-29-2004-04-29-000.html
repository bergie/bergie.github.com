--- title: "Motiva Runs MidCOM" categories: - "business" layout: "post" --- Motiva is using the yamp-based staging/live scripts and the filesystem cache engine from MidCOM 1.2.x. The site is available in three languages: Finnish, English and Swedish.

The server signature is also quite interesting:
Apache/1.3.29 (Unix) Midgard/1.6.0/Repository/MultiLang PHP/4.2.3

MidCOM provides many nice new features for the site, including:

  • Clean, pronounceable URLs
  • "Edit this page" functionality
  • RSS news feeds
  • New site components
The site conversion from the old NemeinNavBar system to MidCOM was handled by Henri Kaukola from Nemein.
